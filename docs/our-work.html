LIBRARY OUTREACH 
Library Outreach Program
We worked with 7 libraries in Baltimore City to teach local children fundamental STEM topics. Over the past two years, we have conducted 70+ weekly events to demonstrate the applications of these topics. During these STEM events, we present students with challenges to overcome, like building structurally sound skyscrapers and bridges. Many children don’t get to experience hands-on demonstrations of STEM in Baltimore, but our outreach gives children the opportunity to unearth their STEM potential.

In the past year, our program reached 150+ students overall, with many attending multiple events.
 TEAM MENTORING 
Team Mentoring
Our team mentored 3 VEX IQ teams at Roland Park Elementary/Middle School. We helped them design their robots, implement those designs, and program, as well as mentoring the students. We collaborated with other local teams from Bryn Mawr School to provide Roland Park with a large number of volunteers.

Our team mentored Team 18996, S.T.A.T.I.C. on CAD and code. We began in February 2023 and have continued helping since then. Additionally, we helped them manufacture their parts by giving them access to our 3d printers and CNC machine.

Our team also mentored our school’s FTC teams, #11695 and #7988, by hosting CAD workshops, Roadrunner tuning help, and engineering design.
 MISCELLANEOUS 
Miscellaneous
We worked closely with the Student Member of the Board of Education of Baltimore County to enact our changes and advocate for future improvements.

By speaking out at multiple Baltimore County Public School board meetings (October 10, October 24, November 7, January 9th and January 23), we advocated for reinstating funds for CCBC-BCPS dual enrollment, increasing the amount of STEM Magnet Schools, and providing more half day STEM explorations.

We partnered with Special Education Equal Development Society (SEEDS) to showcase our robot and 3D-Printing to kids with developmental setbacks at club meetings. We also showcased this at a senior home in Ellicott City.
