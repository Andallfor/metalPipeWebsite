Piping.

Here at Metal Pipe, we are dedicated to the pursuit of one singular goal: piping people. We will not rest until every person, young or old, man or woman, alive or dead, has been fully piped. We shall not back down in the face of adversity, relent under a torrent of pressure, nor cease until we have tasted the sweet metal of victory. That is the Metal Pipe promise.
